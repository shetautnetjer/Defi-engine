# Evidence Rollup Turn

Goal: convert scattered artifacts into normalized evidence.

Do:
- scan SQL run/session/proposal/comparison tables
- scan QMD/JSON receipts
- normalize evidence units
- assign maturity stage
- summarize corpus health
- summarize trading loop health
- rank failure families
- recommend one next batch

Do not:
- promote
- edit risk
- edit policy
- change strategy eligibility
- add providers

Required files:
- `evidence_units.jsonl`
- `summary.json`
- `report.qmd`
